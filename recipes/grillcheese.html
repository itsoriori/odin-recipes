Grilled Cheese

Description

Fast and easy grilled cheese

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Pre-heat pan on midium-low heat
  2. lay butter on pan
  3. lay bread on buttered pan
  4. stack a piece of cheese and cover with another piece of bread
  5. after browning, flip the sandwhich to the otherside and cook till golden-brown
  6. serve
