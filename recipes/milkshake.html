MilkShake

Description

Simple vanilla milkshake!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 tablespoons whipped cream
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. Add the ice cream, milk, and vanilla to a blender, and blend on high for 1 minute.
  2. Add the whipped cream and chocolate sprinkles on top. Serve immediately
