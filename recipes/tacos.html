Tacos

Simple homemade Tacos
  • Ground beef
  • Tortillas
  • Seasoning of choice
  • Beef broth
  • Pico de gallo
  • Guacamole
  • Sour cream
  • Lime
  • Lettuce
  1. On medium heat cook groud beef till slightly brown then add beef broth and spices; keep on heat till fully cooked.
  2. On a seperate pan heat up tortillas
  3. Add ground beef to warm tortillas then add all fresh vegatables and toppings.
